The History of the Mario Series

The world was first introduced to one of the most iconic characters in video game histroy in 1981. Shigeru Miyamoto was the creator of the Mario Series. The Mario Series alone has sold more than 240 million unites. The first game to come out of the series was Super Mario Bro, which was released in Japan on September 13, 1985. The game was relased in North America later that year and became one of the most popular video games of all time. The Super Mario Bros game sold more than 40 million copies for the classic Nintendo Entertainment System(NES).
